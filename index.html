Our Specialized Services

Mental Health Counseling Programs

Cultivating healing, mental wellness, and personal growth in a supportive setting.

Child & Teen Counseling

Counseling for ages 7 and older, helping youths manage school anxiety, peer struggles, social skills, and self-esteem challenges.

Individual Adult Therapy

Individualized, professional mental health wellness therapy targeting anxiety, depression, major life transitions, and stress management.

Family & Relationship Therapy

Collaborative family dynamic consulting, conflict resolution, parenting support, and premarital counseling guidance.

Love and Logic® Parent Training

Skill-building classes providing practical tools to help parents raise self-confident, motivated children while reducing household stress.

Social & Emotional Skill-Building

Group-based and individual coaching designed to develop emotional regulation, empathy, self-awareness, and peer relationship skills.

Intake & Screenings

Comprehensive educational and clinical screenings to identify underlying learning barriers or clinical mental health needs.

Specialized Educational & Tutoring Programs

Developing reading fluency, visual math skills, executive functioning, and home education paths.

Lindamood-Bell® Methods

Customized programs utilizing sensory-cognitive processing for language, math, and reading: Visualizing & Verbalizing® (comprehension), Seeing Stars® (word recognition), LiPS® (phonemic awareness), and Talkies®.

Barton Reading & Spelling

Intensive, research-based Orton-Gillingham system targeted for individuals with dyslexia, teaching spelling, writing, and phonological decoding skills.

On Cloud 9® Math

A visual, sensory-cognitive approach to learning math concepts, helping students visualize word problems, computations, and basic math facts.

Home Education Support

Curriculum planning packages, paperwork guidance (Notice of Intent), record maintenance, and legal consulting with veteran homeschooling advisors.

Test Prep & Traditional Tutoring

Targeted prep courses for SAT and ACT, subject-specific tutoring, academic screenings, and study skill coaching for K-12 students.

Diverse Learner Accommodations

Specialized strategies and learning sessions designed for neurodivergent learners navigating ADHD, autism, or developmental processing delays.

The Breakthroughs Difference

A Coordinated Approach to Success

At Breakthroughs of North Florida, we know that educational achievements and emotional wellbeing are closely linked. When a child struggles to read, spell, or keep up mathematically, it can lead to academic frustration, low self-esteem, and behavioral stress at home.

By coordinating specialized educational tutoring and clinical counseling in a single office, our multidisciplinary team of therapists and educators builds a cohesive roadmap. We aim to help every child, teen, and adult achieve success, one session at a time.
